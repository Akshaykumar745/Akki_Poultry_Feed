About Akki Poultry Feed

Committed to Quality and Excellence

Our Story

Akki Poultry Feed has been serving farmers and poultry entrepreneurs for over 15 years. We started with a simple mission: to provide premium quality feed that helps raise healthy, productive poultry. Today, we're proud to be a trusted name in the poultry feed industry.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, affordable poultry feed that maximizes productivity and ensures the health and well-being of poultry across the region.

Our Vision

To be the leading poultry feed supplier, recognized for innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction in the agriculture industry.

Our Core Values

Quality

Customer Focus

Sustainability

We're committed to environmentally responsible practices in all our operations.
