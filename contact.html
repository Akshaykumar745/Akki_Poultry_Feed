Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?

We accept orders of 1 bag or more. Bulk orders may qualify for special discounts.

Do you provide delivery?

Yes, we provide home delivery for orders within our service area. Delivery charges apply based on location.

Are your products certified?

Yes, all our products are certified and comply with national and international quality standards.

Can I return defective products?

We have a 7-day return policy for defective or damaged products. Please contact us for the process.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes! Special discounts are available for bulk orders. Please contact our sales team for quotes.

How can I track my order?

After login to your account, you can track your orders in the "My Orders" section.
